1:1 video consultation

Get personal help
on your Italy move

A focused 45-minute video call to review your specific situation, identify the right visa pathway, and produce a written action plan you can execute.

When this consultation helps

I take a small number of consultations each week — typically 5–8. The hour pays for itself when you're stuck on a question the articles can't fully answer: whether your specific income mix qualifies for the ERV, whether your great-grandfather's 1923 naturalisation breaks your jure sanguinis line, whether the flat tax regime makes sense given your IRS situation.

I'm not a lawyer or a tax advisor and I don't pretend to be. What I can do is sit down with you, look at your situation, and tell you: (a) what path you're actually on, (b) what to do next, and (c) when you need a specific Italian professional and who to call.

What the session covers

I'll review your situation in advance using the intake form. The 45-minute call is structured as:

  • 10 minutes — your situation, your timeline, your goals
  • 15 minutes — visa pathway analysis: which visa fits and why
  • 10 minutes — tax and residency implications you need to plan for
  • 5 minutes — the realistic timeline and budget
  • 5 minutes — your questions

Within 48 hours after the call, you receive a written action plan — typically 2–4 pages — summarising the path, the documents to start gathering, the professionals to contact, and the realistic timeline.

What it doesn't cover

I cannot:

  • File your visa application or any government paperwork on your behalf
  • Provide legal advice (engage an avvocato for that)
  • Provide tax advice (engage a commercialista for that)
  • Promise outcomes — Italian consulates and questuras make their own decisions

For all of those, I will recommend the specific professionals I trust. I do not take referral fees and my recommendations are independent.

How it works

1 — Book your slot

Pick an available time on my calendar (Eastern time + Central Europe time, both visible).

2 — Complete the intake form

I'll send a short intake form (5 minutes to complete). I review your situation before the call so we don't waste the 45 minutes on basics.

3 — Join the video call

Zoom or Google Meet. English or Italian. I record the call for your records (with your consent).

4 — Receive your action plan

Within 48 hours after the call, by email.

Questions about consultations

Do you take refunds? Yes — full refund within 24 hours of booking, before the intake form is reviewed. After that, no refund but the session can be rescheduled.

Can you do this in Italian? Yes, my Italian is conversationally fluent.

What if I need ongoing help? I offer a 3-call package at a discounted rate — useful for clients planning a complex move over 6–12 months.
