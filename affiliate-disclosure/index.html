Affiliate Disclosure

Some links on Italopedia are affiliate links. Here's our full disclosure — what they are, which companies they go to, and how we keep editorial independence.

Last updated: May 15, 2026

1. What an affiliate link is

An affiliate link is a special URL that lets a company know we referred you. If you purchase through one, the company pays Italopedia a commission. You pay exactly the same price as if you went directly.

2. How we use affiliate links

Italopedia uses affiliate links sparingly, with the following rules:

  1. We only link to products and services we have personally used or that we would recommend without the affiliate relationship.
  2. Affiliate boxes are clearly labelled "Recommended" or "Sponsored" — never disguised as editorial.
  3. Editorial content (articles) is unaffected by commercial relationships. We refuse paid placement and editorial coverage decisions are made independent of affiliate considerations.
  4. We refuse affiliate relationships with any product we would not recommend on the merits.

3. Companies we currently have affiliate relationships with

  • Cigna Global Health Insurance — visa-compliant health insurance for expats
  • AXA International — alternative international health insurance
  • Allianz Care — international health insurance
  • Wise (formerly TransferWise) — international money transfers
  • Idealista — Italian property search
  • Italki / Preply — Italian language tutoring
  • Amazon Associates — book recommendations only

The list is updated quarterly. If we add a partner, it is disclosed here within 30 days.

4. What affiliates do not buy

  • Coverage in editorial articles
  • Mention by name in reviews unless we would mention them anyway
  • Favorable comparisons
  • Suppression of unfavorable comparisons

If Cigna ever gets worse than AXA, we will say so — affiliate relationship or not.

5. The FTC disclosure

For US readers: in accordance with FTC guidelines, this constitutes our affiliate disclosure. Italopedia is a participant in affiliate programs and receives compensation for purchases made through affiliate links.
