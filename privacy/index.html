Privacy Policy

How Italopedia collects, uses, and protects your personal information. GDPR-compliant and written in plain English.

Last updated: May 15, 2026

1. Who we are

Italopedia ("the Site", "we", "us") is operated by Fabrizio Boggio, an independent publisher based in Piedmont, Italy. Email: info@italopedia.com.

2. What information we collect

We collect only the information you actively provide. Specifically:

  • Newsletter signups: Your name, email address, and the relocation stage you select. Used to send the weekly newsletter and the relocation checklist PDF.
  • Consultations: The information you submit on the intake form, used to prepare for your consultation.
  • Shop purchases: Name, email, billing details, and the product purchased. Processed by Stripe.
  • Comments & contact forms: Anything you choose to send us.
  • Analytics: We use a privacy-respecting analytics provider (Plausible) that does not use cookies and does not collect personal data.

3. What we don't collect

We do not use Google Analytics. We do not use Facebook Pixel. We do not sell your data, ever. We do not run third-party advertising on this site. We do not share your email with anyone.

4. Legal basis (GDPR Art. 6)

We process your data on the basis of: your consent (newsletter, marketing); the performance of a contract (purchases, consultations); and our legitimate interest in operating and improving the Site (analytics).

5. Your rights under GDPR

You have the right to: access the data we hold about you; correct it; erase it; object to processing; restrict processing; and port it to another service. Email info@italopedia.com to exercise any of these rights — we respond within 30 days.

6. Data retention

  • Newsletter data: retained until you unsubscribe, plus 12 months for analytics
  • Purchase records: 10 years (required by Italian law)
  • Consultation records: 24 months
  • Contact form messages: 24 months

7. Cookies

See our Cookie Policy. We use only essential cookies plus the cookieless Plausible analytics.

8. Third-party services

We use the following services that process your data:

  • Beehiiv — newsletter delivery
  • Stripe — payment processing
  • Calendly — consultation booking
  • Plausible — privacy-respecting analytics

Each of these services has its own privacy policy. They are GDPR-compliant.

9. Changes to this policy

We will notify subscribers by email of any material changes to this policy. The current version is always at this URL.

10. Contact

Questions about your data? Email info@italopedia.com.
