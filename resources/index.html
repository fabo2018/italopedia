M
Ministero degli Affari Esteri
The Italian foreign ministry. Visa types, consulate jurisdictions, official forms. Italian and English.

A
Agenzia delle Entrate
Italian Revenue Agency. Tax forms, codice fiscale online application, treaty texts.
agenziaentrate.gov.it →
P
Polizia di Stato
National police. Permesso di soggiorno status tracking, questura locations, immigration office.
poliziadistato.it →
S
Ministero della Salute
Italian health ministry. SSN information, regional ASL portals, vaccination schedules.
salute.gov.it →
G
Gazzetta Ufficiale
The official journal where Italian laws are published. The source for legal currency.
gazzettaufficiale.it →
N
Consiglio Nazionale del Notariato
Italian notary council. Find a notaio, property law explainers, fee schedules.
notariato.it →
